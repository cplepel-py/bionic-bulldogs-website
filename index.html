About Us

We are the Bionic Bulldogs, an FTC robotics team from Riverside-Brookfield High School in Riverside, Illinois. The 2019-2020 season was our third year competing in the FIRST Technology Challenge, and we won first place in the Inspire award in the Chicago-North League. Last year, we placed 5th in our league, and we won the Rookie of the Year award our first year. Each year, we strive to find innovative ideas to create a successful robot, always learning from our mistakes to do better than we did before. Learn more about our robot for the current season and meet our dedicated team.

We also like to be a positive member of our community. Last year, we set up a tool library program in our local community, and this year, we plan to create a guide for new FIRST Tech Challenge teams. Learn more about our community outreach.

Of course, we could not be successful without some help, and we are very thankful to our generous sponsors.
